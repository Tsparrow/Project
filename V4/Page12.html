Staying in the smithy for so long has made you overheated, and the harrowing climb and noxious atmosphere only further saps your strength. Just before reaching the top, you stash your bag, the sword, and your outerwear, including your gloves behind a boulder to retrieve on the climb back. You sway dizzily as you approach the massive mouth of the volcano. A sputtering flame pops, startling you, and you stagger slightly. The ground crumbles around your clumsy feet. You scramble to recover as the ground all around you seems to disintegrate, falling into the volcano. At the last second, your hands catch onto the rock. The recent activity of the volcano has resulted in fresh rock that has not yet had the time to cool. The heated rock burns the flesh of one of your hands. Jerking back, you dangle on the precipice with only one drained arm and an inevitable fate. With a scream, you fall into a hot bath of magma.
