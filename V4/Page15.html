Climbing down carefully, you enter the fissure in the rock. Following the sound of voices, you creep down what appears to be a lava vent. Entering a cavernous chamber, you duck behind an outcropping to observe.

"There's talk of the blade's return to the village," a cloaked soldier reports to a man who appears to his leader.

"And the chosen one?" he asks.

"No one knows for sure."

The leader of the Crimson Hood walks toward a bubbling pool of lava. A figure made of magma and fire emerges. A fire elemental! You'd heard they existed, but never seen one. Their loyalty was said to be as mercurial as their form, and their allegiances could shift in an instant. A legion of brimstone soldiers emerge from the magma behind them.
