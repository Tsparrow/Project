The smithy throws down his tools and pulls aside his shirt to show the mark of the paladins. "I knew you were the chosen one!" He implores you to join the forces of good and defeat the corrupt Crimson Hood.

You accept.

Leaving his shop in the care of another, the smithy, Hrakmar the Skull-crusher (you don't ask about the name) joins you and becomes both your mentor and brother-in-arms. You wage war against the Crimson Hood. The Blade of the Chosen One enables you to kill with one blow, and you unify the broken empire.
