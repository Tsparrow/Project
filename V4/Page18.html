After winding through the village to an out-of-the-way hut, you join her in a cup of punch. Her smile widens after you take a deep swig. You assume she's impressed with your ability to hold your drink, right up until the world spins.

You awaken to find an empty hut and your sword gone. On the table is a parchment with a strange marking on it. As you stumble from the hut, you see another villager who identifies the mark as that of the Crimson Hood. The villager spits on the ground after saying their name.

In the days to come, eruptions increased in frequency and magnitude; the heat intensified; and soon the village found itself buried in ash.
