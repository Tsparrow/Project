The Fire Elemental kneels before you, recognizing from your sword that you are the chosen one, meant to lead them. The brimstone army is yours to command. You wage war against the Crimson Hood with your mighty army and reclaim the kingdom. After you deliver the final blow in this war of light and darkness, the brimstone returns from whence they came. The world is at peace. As you travel to the liege of the kingdom, Ashbringer disappears. Rather than search for it, you accept that it will choose a bearer again, if it is needed.
