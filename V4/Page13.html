He directs you to his forge and you watch him create a sword. Even though this was not quite what you were intending, you soon find yourself forging your first nail. He helps you form the metal, and soon you find your technique. As you ask questions and grow more interested, the smithy sadly recognizes that you are not the chosen one. He takes you on as an apprentice and, gradually, the bond between you grows stronger as he becomes like a father to you. One day, the sword you brought with you goes missing. Accepting that it was meant to be, you live happy and content in the shadow of the volcano, waiting for the true chosen one.
