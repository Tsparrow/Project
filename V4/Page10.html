You quickly tell him you were looking for the chamber pot. As he stares at you, you begin to have hope that he'll buy your excuse.

Instead, he asks, "How dumb do you think I am?"

You think it's best not to answer. He grabs your arm and drags you before the contingent's leader who likewise doesn't believe your story. While muttering dire threats, you're thrown in a cell. Aha! All is not lost. If you keep your head, you might yet escape this makeshift prison. Looking around, you attempt to no avail to find tools to escape. Exhausted, you fall asleep.

At the crack of dawn, you're yanked out of bed and, within minutes, all hope is lost, and you lose your head. Literally.
