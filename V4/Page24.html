With your brute rush, sword drawn, you attack. Your sword is unlike any you've ever wielded. A single blow kills soldier after soldier as you make your way to the leader.

"The Chosen One!" he snarls before engaging you in battle. He avoids your blade while advancing and forces you back against the cavern wall. With a powerful cry, you bash him with all your might. He falls backwards, defenseless. With one last sundering strike, you finish him.

Only the fire elemental and its army remain. They stare at you, waiting for you to act.
